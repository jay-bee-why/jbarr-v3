My work

Prior to my current role as a Senior Product Designer at Carbon Five, I was principal UX designer and information architect at Media Temple, a web hosting and support provider based in Los Angeles. By its nature web hosting is highly technical, but I took pride in making interactions with our products easy and delightful for users.

I’ve been lucky to work on a wide range of cool projects like:

  • Multiple responsive websites with over a million views a month, including Media Temple's website and its AccountCenter.
  • A responsive, animated ad appearing on CodePen.
  • A sales-generating tool to help business owners analyze their website's performance.
