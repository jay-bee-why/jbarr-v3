My work

I’m currently a Senior Product Designer at Carbon Five, where I work with startups and enterprise clients alike to test and launch products in a variety of business domains. In a given day, I might sketch, wireframe, prototype, facilitate a workshop, or manage a backlog of features.

Before that, I was the principal UX designer (and occasional front-end developer) at Media Temple, a web hosting provider based in Los Angeles.

I’ve been lucky to work on a wide range of cool projects like:

  • Multiple responsive websites with over a million views a month, including Media Temple's website and its AccountCenter.
  • A responsive, animated ad appearing on CodePen.
  • A sales-generating tool to help business owners analyze their website’s performance.
