My work

Prior to my current role as a Senior Product Designer at Carbon Five, I was principal UX designer and information architect at Media Temple, a web hosting and support provider based in Los Angeles. By its nature web hosting is highly technical, but I took pride in making interactions with our products easy and delightful for users.

I've been lucky to work on a wide range of cool projects like:

  • Multiple responsive websites with over a million views a month, including Media Temple's website and its AccountCenter.
  • A responsive, animated ad appearing on CodePen.
  • A sales-generating tool to help business owners analyze their website's performance.

Community KnowledgeBase

UX Research, Wireframes

The Media Temple KnowledgeBase is a curated library of self-help articles maintained by our Community Support team. It was due for a front-end update and I took the opportunity to improve its UX. I considered the existing KnowledgeBase's strengths, interpreted Google Analytics data, and evaluated the Zendesk API capabilities to improve layout and content hierarchy. Users can now more readily find the content they're searching and browsing for.

Shopping Cart and Checkout Flow

UX Research, Wireframes, UI Design

Media Temple recently rebuilt its purchase process site-wide in order to accommodate a traditional "shopping cart" style checkout process, as well as a brand-new log-in/sign-up step, both of which were lacking in previous iterations. I leveraged my knowledge of our back-end processes to create a flow that users were familiar with, while introducing new steps like mid-checkout authentication.
