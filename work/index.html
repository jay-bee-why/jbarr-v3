My work

I’m currently the UX Architect at Media Temple, a web hosting and support provider based in Los Angeles. I’m the principal UX designer and information architect, as well as a stakeholder in every product the customer touches. By its nature web hosting is technical, but I take pride in making interactions with our products easy and delightful for users.

I've done a bunch of stuff, including:

  • I rebuilt Media Temple’s LiveChat widget from the ground up, which allows sales and support to chat with customers from our website.
  • Conceived the process for Media Temple users to merge multiple accounts.
  • Designed our new Unified Cart, allowing users to purchase multiple products at once in a traditional online shopping cart experience.
  • Planned and designed the user on-boarding experiences for our new Google Apps products and our relaunch of our Premium WordPress product.
  • Worked with Software Engineers to future-proof our internal ordering system by allowing users to onboard with future, yet to be developed products.
  • Planned and designed the integration of CloudTech security and monitoring informational panels throughout the AccountCenter, which utilize Panopta and SiteLock APIs, respectively.
