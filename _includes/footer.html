About

Justin Barr Young designs and develops user experiences for web and mobile products.

He's the principal UX designer and information architect (and occasional front-end developer) at Media Temple, a webhosting and support provider. He designs and guides the creation of user experiences that make Media Temple's products easy and valuable for users. On the side, he designs and develops Two Words For, an online arts and literary magazine (and a constantly-changing work in progress).
